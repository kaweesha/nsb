Cancellations

  • Cancellations should be done 5 days before the reserved date. Therefore another person who is waiting will get a chance to do a reservation.
  • Cancellations can be done by contacting the welfare manager of NSB/nearest NSB branch manager/second officer.
  • If you do your cancellation 5 days before the reserved date the payments you have done will be hold back for your next journey.
  • Payments are not refundable.
  • If a cancellation is not done 5 days before the reserved date, payments will not be hold back and the waiting list will not be notified.
  • Please try to keep your cancellation rate low since we are keeping track of the cancellation history. You might be prohibited to use circuit bungalows.
