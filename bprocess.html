Booking Process

  • All NSB employers, employees and pensioners are eligible to place reservations.
  • Check the reservation calendar before you book a date. See if it is available or not.
  • When you are ready to do your reservation you should contact the welfare manager of NSB/nearest NSB branch manager/second officer. They will place your reservation if the date is available.
  • You should book your bungalow 14 days before the date you are reserving.
  • You should pay for the reservation within 7 days after the reservation has been placed. Unless the reservation will be cancelled. An SMS will be sent to you reminding the payments on the 4th day.
  • You can pay for the reservation from any NSB branch and it should be debited to the welfare division of NSB. (Payments are not refundable)
  • Show the receipt to the welfare manager of NSB/nearest NSB branch manager/second officer and confirm your reservation.
  • If the date you search is not available you can add yourself to the waiting list. Contact the welfare manager of NSB/nearest NSB branch manager/second officer to add yourself to a waiting list. If the reservation on the date you are waiting was cancelled you will get a chance to do your reservation.
  • Important: If someone from the executive board needs to do a reservation they can cancel your reservation anytime and you will be notified about the cancellation through an SMS. (The payments you have done will be hold back for your next journey.)
  • Please contact the welfare manager of NSB/nearest NSB branch manager/second officer for more information.
